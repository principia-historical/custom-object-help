Click the target button to set what resource the vendor accepts.

<b><big>Socket information:</big></b>
<b><tt>IN0</tt></b>: Activate
<b><tt>IN1</tt></b>: Reset
<b><tt>OUT0</tt></b>: Bought one item
<b><tt>OUT1</tt></b>: Fraction of items remaining
